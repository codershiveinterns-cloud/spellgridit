Privacy Policy

How SpellGridit handles inquiry and website information.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website or contact SpellGridit about a website, automation, SEO, branding, or digital growth project.

Information we collect

The current website is a static marketing site. Its front-end lead forms display a confirmation message in the browser and do not send submissions to SpellGridit unless a form provider, email service, CRM, or backend is connected later.

If you contact SpellGridit by email or through any connected inquiry form, SpellGridit may receive the details you choose to provide, such as your name, email address, company name, website URL, project type, budget range, timeline, and message.

How information is used

Inquiry information is used to understand your request, respond to your message, recommend a service or package, prepare project scopes, and communicate about potential or active work.

SpellGridit does not use inquiry information for unrelated automated profiling, and does not sell personal information.

Payments and sensitive data

This website does not process payments and does not ask for payment card numbers, government identification numbers, health information, or account passwords. If a project requires payment, billing terms and payment processing should be handled through a separate written agreement and a secure payment provider.

Service providers

This site may be hosted by a third-party hosting provider. If SpellGridit later connects forms, email delivery, analytics, CRM, scheduling, or payment tools, those providers may process information only as needed to deliver their services. Their own privacy practices may also apply.

Data retention

SpellGridit keeps inquiry and project communications only as long as needed to respond, manage business records, support active work, resolve issues, or meet legal and accounting obligations. You may request that SpellGridit delete or update your contact information where applicable.

Security

SpellGridit uses reasonable care to protect information received through business communications. No website, email system, or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your choices

You can choose not to submit optional form fields. You can also contact SpellGridit to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information provided through inquiries, subject to any required business or legal retention needs.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@spellgridit.com.
