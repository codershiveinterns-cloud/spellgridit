Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency about recommendations, tools, and partner links.

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This disclosure explains how SpellGridit may mention, recommend, compare, or link to third-party tools, platforms, hosting services, software, and business resources on this website.

Quick summary

Our recommendation policy

SpellGridit may earn a commission if you click certain third-party links or purchase recommended tools. This does not increase your cost and does not change our commitment to clear, useful recommendations.

  • Some links may be affiliate or referral links.
  • Recommendations are based on practical business use cases.
  • Third-party tools have their own pricing and policies.
  • Always review provider details before purchasing.
01

What affiliate links mean

Some pages, blog posts, guides, emails, or service recommendations from SpellGridit may include affiliate links, referral links, partner links, or tracked links to third-party services.

If you click one of these links and make a purchase, sign up, or start a paid plan, SpellGridit may receive a commission, credit, referral fee, or other benefit from the provider.

02

No extra cost to you

When an affiliate commission applies, it is normally paid by the third-party provider. Your price should not increase because you used an affiliate link from SpellGridit.

Pricing, discounts, renewal charges, taxes, cancellation terms, and subscription rules are controlled by the third-party provider and should be reviewed before purchase.

03

How recommendations are made

SpellGridit may recommend tools that support website projects, hosting, forms, analytics, CRM workflows, email marketing, automation, SEO, design, security, or business operations.

Recommendations are intended to help businesses choose practical tools for their goals. We aim to consider usability, reliability, support needs, pricing clarity, integration fit, and project requirements.

04

Editorial independence

Affiliate relationships may influence which tools are mentioned, but they do not guarantee a positive recommendation. SpellGridit aims to keep recommendations useful, relevant, and aligned with the client or reader’s needs.

No provider can purchase a guaranteed project result, ranking, review outcome, or service recommendation unless that relationship is clearly identified.

05

Third-party responsibility

Third-party websites, hosting providers, domain services, CRM tools, automation platforms, payment processors, analytics tools, plugins, and software products are controlled by their own companies.

SpellGridit is not responsible for third-party pricing changes, outages, data practices, support quality, billing issues, account decisions, product updates, or terms and policy changes.

06

Client project recommendations

During website, automation, CRM, SEO, or marketing projects, SpellGridit may suggest third-party tools that fit the scope. Some recommendations may include affiliate or referral relationships.

Clients are encouraged to ask questions, compare alternatives, and approve any paid tool before setup or purchase. Final tool selection should match the client’s budget, business needs, and compliance requirements.
