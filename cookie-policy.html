Cookie Policy

How SpellGridit uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This policy explains the current cookie status of the SpellGridit website and how that may change if analytics, forms, or third-party tools are connected.

Current cookie use

The current SpellGridit website is a static front-end site and does not intentionally set non-essential cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. The site does not currently include a tracking script or cookie banner because no optional tracking cookies are active.

Technical storage

Your browser, device, or hosting provider may use basic technical storage, caching, security checks, or server logs to deliver the website. These functions help pages load, keep the site available, and protect the hosting environment.

Future tools

If SpellGridit later connects analytics, embedded media, form providers, scheduling tools, CRM tools, chat widgets, payment processors, or advertising platforms, those services may use cookies or similar technologies. SpellGridit should update this policy before enabling optional tracking or marketing cookies.

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block cookies, delete stored cookies, or receive alerts before cookies are stored. Blocking some technical storage may affect how certain websites load or function.
