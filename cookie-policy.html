Cookie Policy

How SpellGridit uses cookies and similar technologies.

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This policy explains the current cookie status of the SpellGridit website, what technical storage may be used, and how cookies may apply if analytics, forms, scheduling, or third-party tools are connected in the future.

Cookie overview

Clear cookie use, no unnecessary tracking.

The current SpellGridit website is a static marketing website and does not intentionally set optional advertising or analytics cookies.

  • No active ad tracking cookies.
  • No optional analytics cookies currently active.
  • Technical storage may support loading and security.
  • Browser settings can control cookies.
01

What cookies are

Cookies are small files stored on your device by websites. They can help websites remember preferences, support login sessions, measure performance, improve security, or enable third-party tools.

Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, caching, and server logs. These tools can serve different purposes depending on how a website is built and what services are connected.

02

Current cookie use

The current SpellGridit website is a static front-end site and does not intentionally set non-essential cookies, advertising cookies, or optional analytics cookies.

The site does not currently include a tracking script or cookie banner because optional tracking cookies are not active at this time.

03

Technical storage

Your browser, device, hosting provider, or security layer may use basic technical storage, caching, server logs, or security checks to deliver the website correctly.

These functions can help pages load faster, keep the website available, detect technical issues, prevent abuse, and protect the hosting environment. Some technical storage may be necessary for normal website operation.

04

Analytics cookies

SpellGridit does not currently use optional analytics cookies on this static website. If analytics tools are connected later, they may help understand visitor activity such as popular pages, traffic sources, device types, and performance issues.

If optional analytics cookies are enabled in the future, SpellGridit should update this policy and provide any required notice or consent options based on applicable requirements.

05

Marketing and advertising cookies

The website does not currently use advertising pixels, retargeting cookies, or marketing tracking cookies. These tools may be used by advertising platforms to measure campaigns or show relevant ads across other websites.

If SpellGridit later uses advertising or retargeting tools, this policy should be updated to explain the purpose of those tools and how visitors can manage their choices.

06

Third-party tools

If SpellGridit later connects embedded media, form providers, scheduling tools, CRM tools, chat widgets, payment processors, cloud services, analytics, or advertising platforms, those third parties may use cookies or similar technologies.

Third-party services are controlled by their own providers. Their cookies, retention rules, privacy practices, and opt-out options may be described in their own policies.

07

Forms and inquiry tools

The current front-end forms display confirmation messages in the browser and do not submit information unless a backend, email service, CRM, or form provider is connected later.

If a form provider is added, it may use cookies or technical storage for spam prevention, session handling, form security, or analytics. SpellGridit should update this policy if that changes the website’s cookie behavior.

08

Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block cookies, delete stored cookies, clear site data, or receive alerts before cookies are stored.

Blocking some technical storage may affect how certain websites load or function. Because SpellGridit currently does not intentionally use optional tracking cookies, most cookie control is handled through your browser settings.

09

Browser controls

Cookie settings are usually available in your browser’s privacy or security settings. You may be able to manage cookies globally or for specific websites.

Common controls include deleting cookies, blocking third-party cookies, clearing cached data, using private browsing modes, or changing tracking protection settings.

10

Updates to this policy

SpellGridit may update this Cookie Policy when the website changes, third-party tools are added, analytics are enabled, forms are connected, or legal requirements change.

The “Last updated” date at the top of this page shows when the policy was most recently revised.
