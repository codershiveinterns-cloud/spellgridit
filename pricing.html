What affects price

Pricing depends on scope, not just page count.

A professional website project can include design, content, development, forms, SEO structure, integrations, performance work, and launch support. The final price depends on how much detail and customization your project needs.

01

Content readiness

Projects move faster when copy, images, offers, and brand assets are ready before design begins.

02

Page complexity

Custom layouts, long service pages, case studies, blogs, and landing pages affect scope.

03

Integrations

Forms, analytics, CRM tools, scheduling, automation, and third-party services may add work.

04

Timeline

Rush timelines, extra revisions, or incomplete materials can affect delivery and pricing.

FAQ

Common pricing questions.

How long does a website take?

Most starter sites take 1-3 weeks depending on content readiness, feedback speed, page count, and revision needs.

Can you redesign my existing site?

Yes. SpellGridit can refresh structure, visuals, copy, performance, and mobile experience while keeping your core offer intact.

Do you provide copywriting?

Yes, packages can include headline, section, and service-page copy support depending on the selected scope.

Can this connect to a CRM?

Yes, the static form UI can be connected to a form service, CRM, automation tool, or custom endpoint later.

Is hosting included?

Hosting is not included by default, but SpellGridit can guide deployment to common static hosting platforms.

Can I start small?

Yes. Many projects start with a focused launch site and expand with more pages, content, and systems later.
